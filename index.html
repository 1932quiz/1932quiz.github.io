Who would you be in 1932 Germany?

Made by Seva Gaskov.

In this test, you will be asked a series of questions that will determine which poltical party you would have likely joined if you lived in 1932 Germany. You will be asked questions about the German society and economy, as well as the Great War. Your answers will be used to map where you fit on a political spectrum and which party your beliefs most closely resemble.
